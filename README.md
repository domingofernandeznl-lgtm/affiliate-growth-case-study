## The Challenge

## My Approach

**1. Designed a Scalable Creator Program**
- Researched and identified niche influencers across YouTube, education, and music tech.
- Built onboarding workflows, content guidelines, and tiered incentive structures.

**2. Integrated Attribution and Tracking**
- Implemented Tapfiliate and UTM-based tracking.
- Partnered with BI and product teams to refine attribution using AppsFlyer.

**3. Created a High-Conversion Funnel**
- Collaborated with designers and data teams to test multiple creative formats.
- Focused on short-form educational content + long-tail YouTube partnerships.

**4. Optimized Through Experimentation**
- Applied rapid testing cycles to improve ROI.
- Monitored channel-level performance to reallocate budgets efficiently.
